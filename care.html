DrySense is a smart incontinence monitoring device specifically designed for individuals in wheelchairs.

As caregivers ourselves, we know how challenging it is to care for a loved one. We understand the exhaustion, the long sleepless nights, the chronic stress, and the shame and stigmatization of incontinence. We understand the financial burden incontinence places on families. We understand how caring for others often involves forgetting about ourselves.

That is why we invented DrySense. Our goal is to restore the confidence, satisfaction, and bond you feel while caring for a loved one. Our goal is to save you time, money and radically improve BOTH caregivers and care receivers' quality of life.

DrySense is an incontinence monitoring device that alerts caregivers when a patient urinates or defecates. The sensor is embedded or attached to a patient's wheelchair cushion and monitors the local environment for rapid fluctuations in temperature and humidity. When a person experiences a void event, the temperature and humidity around the pelvic region spikes and the sensor sends an alert to the care provider.

  • The device easily installs to any wheelchair cushion.
  • Monitors for void events and alerts caregivers when brief changes are required..
  • Quick battery charging and only requires cleaning when in contact with void excrement.

DrySense monitors the patient's local environment for fluctuations in humidity and temperature every 30seconds. When a void event occurs, DrySense alerts caregivers simultaneously to ensure prompt care is received.

DrySense's system is unintrusive and allows caregivers to move patients freely. The battery is long-lasting (3 days) and recharges within (x). Cleaning the device is only required if it's in direct contact with void excrement (very rare given the device is under a seating cushion).

Designed by Care Experts for Anyone to Use.

DrySense's intuitive application design allows anyone to use the device with confidence and ease.

The application notifies caregivers when a void event has occurred and sends reminders if a change is not conducted.

Application logs void event history, response time and provides users with analytics to improve care.

Monitoring patients is as easy as checking your phone.

Improves Patient Care

DrySense Gives a voice to the silent. By taking the guessing out of incontinence monitoring, DrySense reduces void event response time, increases patient comfort, and reduces incontinence-related complications.

Save Time and Energy

Caregiving should improve your relationship with loved ones, not tear it apart. DrySense makes caring for loved ones easier by reducing the time and energy required to manage incontinence.

Save money

From buying absorbent briefs and pads to hiring professional care, incontinence places a heavy financial burden on families. Moreover, secondary incontinence-related infections and injury such as UTI, skin breakdown, wounds, and pressure injuries, add unexpected expenses to an already stretched budget. DrySense saves families money by reducing false-positive brief changes and reducing incontinence-related infection and injury.
